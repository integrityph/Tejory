Road Map

  1. NFT Integration

    Tejory will add support for non-fungible tokens (NFTs), enabling users to store, view, and manage their NFT collections securely. This will bring the world of digital art, collectibles, and other NFT-based assets directly into Tejory’s platform, further expanding its versatility.

    September 10, 2025

  2. Buy Crypto

    Tejory will integrate a feature allowing users to purchase cryptocurrency directly within the app using a credit card. This will streamline the process of acquiring digital assets, making it easier for both new and experienced investors to enter the crypto space.

    August 10, 2025

  3. Swap coins using DEX

    Tejory will implement decentralized exchange (DEX) functionality, allowing users to swap a wide range of cryptocurrencies directly from the app. This feature will enable users to trade coins without the need for third-party platforms, increasing efficiency and maintaining full control over their assets.

    July 10, 2025

  4. BNB Integration

    Binance Coin (BNB) and tokens on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) will be incorporated into Tejory’s wallet system. This will empower users to interact with the Binance ecosystem and BSC tokens, providing more flexibility and opportunities for decentralized finance (DeFi) users.

    June 10, 2025

  5. Solana Integration

    Solana’s blockchain will be integrated into the Tejory platform, allowing users to store SOL and other Solana-based tokens. This will broaden the scope of supported cryptocurrencies and provide users with the ability to manage assets across multiple high-speed and low-cost blockchain networks.

    May 10, 2025

  6. Ethereum Integration

    Tejory will integrate Ethereum into its platform, enabling users to manage and store ETH and ERC-20 tokens. This will provide Tejory users with broader blockchain support, expanding their ability to manage various assets on Ethereum’s network securely and seamlessly.

    April 10, 2025

  7. Public Market Availability

    Following the successful beta testing phase, Tejory will be officially available on the public market. The platform will be fully operational, and users can begin using the hardware wallet, engaging in secure transactions, and managing their digital assets with the complete suite of Tejory's tools.

    March 10, 2025

  8. Open Beta Testing

    Tejory will enter an open beta phase, where selected users can test the platform and provide feedback. This stage will ensure that all features, including wallet imports, blockchain support, and security protocols, function optimally. It will allow users to experience the platform's performance before its full public release.

    January 10, 2025

  9. Tejory Website Latest

    The Tejory website will introduce users to the platform's features, including wallet integration, multi-blockchain support, and secure storage. It will offer guides, tutorials, and customer support to ensure an easy onboarding experience.

    June 19, 2024

  10. Tejory Idea

    Tejory's first phase focuses on creating a seamless, secure platform combining hardware and software wallets. This ensures offline storage with easy online access for daily transactions. Key steps include developing a hardware wallet prototype, ensuring private key security, and building a platform for both casual investors and experienced traders.

    May 2024
